About TherapTube

TherapTube is a video-based platform built to support occupational therapists and clients through structured and personalized therapy content.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between therapy and accessibility by making rehabilitation videos easily available, trackable, and engaging for clients everywhere.

Who We Serve

  • Therapists – to upload and manage therapy content
  • Clients – to watch, track, and complete therapeutic activities

Created By

This platform was designed by Samuel Peprah (Atom De Legend) to serve clients with disabilities, improve engagement, and support occupational therapy practices in Africa and beyond.
